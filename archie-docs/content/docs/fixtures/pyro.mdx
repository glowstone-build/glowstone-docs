Glowstone simulates two stage pyro devices: **CO2 jets** (rising white fog plumes) and **cold-spark machines** (golden spark fountains). They are placed like fixtures, aim along their nozzle, and are driven by an inline DMX patch — or free-run at a manual level for previz.

## The two kinds

<Tabs items={['CO2 Jet', 'Cold Spark']}>
  <Tab value="CO2 Jet">
    A fast, dense, short-lived **white fog column** that rises and catches coloured stage light. It is alpha-blended and **lit** (not emissive) — the colour comes from the beams hitting it, with an optional faint nozzle tint. The plume billows up to its **Throw** height and lingers for its **Dissipation** time.
  </Tab>
  <Tab value="Cold Spark">
    An upward **golden spark fountain** ("cold pyro" / Sparkular-type): ballistic, additive, velocity-stretched sparks whose colour cools from a hot base to a dim tip along the blackbody curve. It is HDR-emissive and feeds bloom.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Adding a device

Press `Shift`+`A` and pick the **Pyro** category, or drag one in from the **Library**. The nozzle sits at the device origin and the effect fires along local `+Y` (steered by pan/tilt). A freshly placed device free-runs at a visible default so you see the effect immediately; patch it to drive it from a console.

### Built-in devices

| Device | Kind | Defaults |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Cold Spark Machine | Cold spark | 3.5 m apex, 5000 particle cap |
| Cold Spark — Mini | Cold spark | 2.0 m apex, 3000 cap |
| Cold Spark — Moving Head | Cold spark (moving) | 4.0 m apex, 5000 cap |
| CO2 Jet | CO2 | 9 m throw, 1300 cap |
| CO2 Jet — Long Throw | CO2 | 14 m throw, 1500 cap |
| CO2 Jet — Moving (PSYCO2) | CO2 (moving) | 10 m throw, 1400 cap |

## DMX control

Pyro is patched **inline on the device** — not through the fixture patch table — and decoded each frame. Open the Inspector's **DMX** group, tick **Patched**, choose a **Mode**, and set the universe and address.

<TypeTable
  type={{
    Patched: { description: 'Whether the device decodes live DMX. Unpatched devices free-run at the manual Output / Amount.', type: 'toggle', default: 'off' },
    Mode: { description: 'DMX footprint layout (see below).', type: 'Minimal / Rich', default: 'Minimal' },
    Universe: { description: 'DMX universe.', type: '1–63999', default: '1' },
    Address: { description: 'Start channel.', type: '1–512', default: '1' },
  }}
/>

### Footprints

| Kind | Minimal | Rich |
| --- | --- | --- |
| CO2 Jet | 1 ch — Blast (`200`–`255` fires) | 7 ch — Arm, Blast, Intensity, Duration, Pan, Tilt, Speed |
| Cold Spark | 3 ch — Spark | 5 ch — Spark+ |

<Callout type="info">
A patched device only fires when **armed** (the Rich modes carry an arm/safety channel). Unarmed devices stay inert regardless of the blast level; unpatched devices ignore arming and free-run at their manual level.
</Callout>
